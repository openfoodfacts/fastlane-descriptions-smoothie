imikhiqizo ukudla Scan noma ibhakhodi yabo ukuthola idatha izithako, izithasiselo kanye nokudla amaqiniso.

Uhlelo lokusebenza ivumela ukubuka imikhiqizo 537.000 (kuhlanganise 175,000 e-US) kakade oluqukethwe mahhala futhi evulekile semininingwane Vula Ukudla Facts, futhi ukuze ungeze izithombe kanye nemininingwane imikhiqizo ulahlekile.

Ukuze uthole Vula Ukudla Facts phrojekthi, sicela uvakashele https://openfoodfacts.org

Wena uzokwazi ukuthola (ngokuvumelana izinkomba ekhona imikhiqizo):
- carbon lokungcolisa (CO2 kwekhabhoni) kanye emaphaketheni (kanye imiyalelo kabusha),
- Nutriscore (amaphuzu yokudla okunempilo), umsoco, amafutha / lokucuketfwe fat, ayegcwele ama-fatty acid, ama-carbohydrate, ushukela, ama-fiber, amaprotheni usawoti kanye sodium.
- brand, izinto ezixabana nomzimba, amalebula (bio, gluten khulula, vegan, imifino, Halal, kosher ...), ulwazi ukulandeleka (amakhodi emabhokisini imisuka izithako)
- On iwayini besizulu, uzothola lotshwala.
